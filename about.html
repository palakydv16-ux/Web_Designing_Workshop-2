About Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game that allows players to build, explore, survive, and create in a procedurally generated 3D block-based world. The game offers multiple modes including Survival, Creative, Adventure, and Hardcore.

Original Developer: Markus Persson (Notch)

Developed By: Mojang Studios

Published By: Microsoft Studios

Initial Release: 18 November 2011

Latest Version: 1.21 (Java Edition)

Genre: Sandbox, Survival
